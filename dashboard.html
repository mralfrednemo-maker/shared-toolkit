EinMDP Adversarial Critique — Unanimous Verdict (2026-04-10)

REPLACE — The original 3-phase plan was a synthesis program, not a research program.

1. Without a perturbation-measurement loop, every phase is decorative. Understanding ≠ control.
2. Dual-track governance required: Translational Track (safety-gated) + Frontier Track (protected from tractability filtering).
3. Biomarker regime is the most dangerous shared assumption — epigenetic clocks correlate with lifespan but causality is unproven.
4. Mission reframed: "Indefinite Healthspan Extension" — not immortality. "Immortality" destroys milestone logic.
5. ECM cross-linking: real but currently intractable. Placed in Frontier Track, not mainline.
Phase 0 Research — Fresh Findings (Haiku agents, 2026-04-10)

⏱
Biological Age Clocks

›
DunedinPACE — most intervention-responsive clock. Validated in 65+ cohorts. Shows calorie restriction signal.
›
GrimAge v2 — strong mortality predictor. Responds in CALERIE trial.
›
Proteomics clock (204 proteins) — r=0.94 with age. Predicts 18 diseases. No intervention data yet.
›
Critical gap: No clock has demonstrated CAUSAL tracking of lifespan. All are correlates. This is the biomarker mirage problem.

🔬
Model Systems

›
Ex vivo human skin — 3-7 day signal, high throughput, very high human relevance. Best for rapid screening.
›
iPSC-derived organoids — 2-14 days. High human relevance. Lower throughput. Best for organ-level mechanisms.
›
Killifish — 9-18 month lifespan. Whole-organism. Immune senescence mirrors human patterns. 2025: gut FMT from young fish improved health.
›
Golden hybrid: Skin biopsies (3-day rapid screen) + iPSC cardiac/neural organoids (mechanism) = 2-4 week total signal.

💉
Senolytics — Human Trial Status

›
D+Q cognition (STAMINA, n=12) — Safe. MoCA +2.0 pts (exploratory). TNF-α reduction. eBioMedicine 2025.
›
D+Q bone health (Mayo, n=60) — FAILED primary. No sustained bone resorption effect.
›
UBX1325 (Unity, n=52) — Missed non-inferiority at wk 24, achieved at wk 36 vs aflibercept. Eye only.
›
Bottom line: Proof of safety. No large RCT shows biological age reduction. Fisetin Phase II pending.

🧬
Epigenetic Reprogramming

›
OSK vs OSKM: c-Myc dropped due to oncogenesis risk. Cyclic OSK (2 on/5 off) shows zero teratoma over 35 cycles.
›
Altos Labs 2025: Selective gene silencing prevents "cellular drift". Engineered factors 50× more effective than natural OSK.
›
Life Biosciences: FDA cleared Phase I (AAV-OSK) for vision disorders. First human reprogramming trial.
›
Safety horizon: 5-10 years to human efficacy data. Biggest risk: off-target identity drift in non-target tissues.
